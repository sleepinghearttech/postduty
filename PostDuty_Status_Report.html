Custom sticker sheets with QR codes that link to each ambassador's personal discount page. Stick them on hostel notice boards for free marketing.

Badge reels and lanyards weigh under 100g — ship in bubble mailer envelopes at ₹20-25 instead of boxes at ₹36-50. Doubles our margin on single items.

After delivery: Day 1 check-in ("reached safely?"), Day 5 review request, Day 60 win-back offer. Built into our own server at ₹0/month — no paid tools needed.
